Staff roles gate what each person can do once they have access. Admin: every permission, including settings + role management. Moderator / Storyteller: full XP + chronicle management, but not settings or roles. Helper: spend approvals only.

Settings Admin is a separate, stricter gate on chronicle-wide configuration (ruleset, tier budgets, restrictions, XP rules). Even a Lead ST without this flag can't flip rule constants — the grant ships pre-set for existing Lead STs but must be granted explicitly to anyone new.
