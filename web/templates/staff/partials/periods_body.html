Automatic Generation

When on, Enoch learns your rhythm from recent periods — the gap between the last two and how long they stay open — and stamps the next Night a couple of days before it's due. It keeps exactly one period on deck and never auto-opens it; you still press Activate. Needs at least two existing periods to learn from.
